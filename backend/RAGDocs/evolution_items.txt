Evolution Items Guide

Evolution items are special items that cause Pokémon to evolve when used, held, or triggered under specific conditions. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

There are two main types:
1. Items used directly on a Pokémon
2. Items that must be held during trade or leveling

--------------------------------------------------
2. Held Item Evolutions (Trade or Level Up)
--------------------------------------------------

These Pokémon evolve when holding an item and meeting a condition (trade or level up). :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Trade Evolutions with Items:
- Onix + Metal Coat → Steelix
- Scyther + Metal Coat → Scizor
- Seadra + Dragon Scale → Kingdra
- Slowpoke + King’s Rock → Slowking
- Poliwhirl + King’s Rock → Politoed
- Porygon + Upgrade → Porygon2
- Porygon2 + Dubious Disc → Porygon-Z
- Electabuzz + Electirizer → Electivire
- Magmar + Magmarizer → Magmortar
- Rhydon + Protector → Rhyperior

Level-Up While Holding Item:
- Gligar + Razor Fang (night) → Gliscor
- Sneasel + Razor Claw (night) → Weavile
- Happiny + Oval Stone (day) → Chansey

--------------------------------------------------
3. Special Evolution Items
--------------------------------------------------

These items trigger unique evolutions.

Examples:
- Applin + Sweet Apple → Appletun
- Applin + Tart Apple → Flapple
- Applin + Syrupy Apple → Dipplin
- Gimmighoul + 999 coins → Gholdengo

--------------------------------------------------
Evolution Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- Some items are used directly (stones)
- Some must be held during trade or level-up
- Some evolutions depend on time of day or conditions
- Items are consumed after use in most cases
