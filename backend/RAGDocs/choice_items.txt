Choice Items Guide

Choice Items are held items that boost one stat by 50% but lock the Pokémon into the first move it uses until it switches out.

These items are widely used in competitive play because they provide a strong immediate advantage in power or speed.

--------------------------------------------------
Main Choice Items
--------------------------------------------------

Choice Band:
- Increases Attack by 50%
- Locks the user into the first move selected
- Best used on physical attackers

Choice Specs:
- Increases Special Attack by 50%
- Locks the user into the first move selected
- Best used on special attackers

Choice Scarf:
- Increases Speed by 50%
- Locks the user into the first move selected
- Best used to outspeed opponents

--------------------------------------------------
Choice Item Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- The held Pokémon can only use the first move it selects
- The move lock remains until the Pokémon switches out
- Status moves can also cause the user to become locked
- Switching resets the lock
- Choice Items are not consumed after use

--------------------------------------------------
Competitive Use
--------------------------------------------------

- Choice Band is used to maximize physical damage output
- Choice Specs is used to maximize special damage output
- Choice Scarf is used to gain speed control and revenge kill faster threats
- These items are strong but require prediction because the move lock can be exploited
