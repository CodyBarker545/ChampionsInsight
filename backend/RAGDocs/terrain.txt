Terrain Effects Guide

Terrain conditions are battlefield effects that influence grounded Pokémon. 
Only Pokémon that are touching the ground are affected by terrain effects.

Terrain typically lasts for several turns and can be set by moves or abilities.

--------------------------------------------------
Types of Terrain
--------------------------------------------------

Electric Terrain:
- Boosts Electric-type move damage by 30%
- Prevents grounded Pokémon from falling asleep
- Activates abilities such as Surge Surfer

Grassy Terrain:
- Restores 1/16 of max HP each turn for grounded Pokémon
- Boosts Grass-type move damage by 30%
- Reduces damage from certain ground-based moves (e.g., Earthquake)

Misty Terrain:
- Reduces Dragon-type move damage by 50% on grounded targets
- Prevents status conditions on grounded Pokémon

Psychic Terrain:
- Boosts Psychic-type move damage by 30%
- Prevents priority moves from hitting grounded Pokémon

--------------------------------------------------
Terrain Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- Terrain effects last 5 turns
- Duration can be extended to 8 turns with held items (e.g., Terrain Extender)
- Only one terrain can be active at a time
- Flying-type Pokémon and those with Levitate are not affected

--------------------------------------------------
Ability Interaction
--------------------------------------------------

Some abilities activate or benefit from terrain effects.

Examples:
- Electric Surge: Sets Electric Terrain on entry
- Grassy Surge: Sets Grassy Terrain on entry
- Psychic Surge: Sets Psychic Terrain on entry
- Misty Surge: Sets Misty Terrain on entry
