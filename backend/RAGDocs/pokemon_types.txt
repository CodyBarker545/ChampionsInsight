Pokémon Type Effectiveness Guide

This document describes how different Pokémon types interact in battle. 
Each attacking type has strengths (super effective), weaknesses (not very effective), and immunities (no effect) against defending types.

Effectiveness Multipliers:
- 2×: Super effective (strong)
- 0.5×: Not very effective (weak)
- 0×: No effect (immune)
- 1×: Neutral

Dual-Type Note:
If a Pokémon has two types, multiply the effectiveness.
Example:
- 2× and 2× = 4× (very strong)
- 2× and 0.5× = 1× (neutral)
- 0× overrides everything (immune)
