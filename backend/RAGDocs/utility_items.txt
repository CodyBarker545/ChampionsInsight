Utility and Offensive Items Guide

Utility and offensive items are held items that provide unique effects, boost damage, or trigger under specific conditions. 
These items are commonly used in competitive play to gain strategic advantages.

--------------------------------------------------
Main Utility and Offensive Items
--------------------------------------------------

Life Orb:
- Increases damage by 30%
- User loses 10% of max HP after each damaging attack

Focus Sash:
- If the Pokémon is at full HP, it survives a hit with 1 HP
- Only activates once per battle

Weakness Policy:
- Raises Attack and Special Attack by 2 stages when hit by a super-effective move

Expert Belt:
- Boosts damage of super-effective moves by 20%

Choice Lock Reminder:
- Some offensive strategies combine with Choice Items for maximum damage output

--------------------------------------------------
Utility-Based Items
--------------------------------------------------

Rocky Helmet:
- Damages attackers on contact (1/6 of their max HP)

Air Balloon:
- Grants temporary immunity to Ground-type moves
- Pops after taking damage

Heavy-Duty Boots:
- Prevents damage and effects from entry hazards

--------------------------------------------------
Trigger-Based Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- Many utility items activate only under certain conditions
- Some items are consumed after activation (e.g., Focus Sash)
- Some provide permanent effects while held (e.g., Expert Belt)

--------------------------------------------------
Competitive Use
--------------------------------------------------

- Life Orb is used for maximum damage output at the cost of HP
- Focus Sash ensures survival for fragile Pokémon
- Weakness Policy is used to turn defense into offense
- Expert Belt rewards targeting weaknesses
- Utility items help control hazards, positioning, and matchups
