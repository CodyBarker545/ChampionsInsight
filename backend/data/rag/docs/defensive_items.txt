Defensive Items Guide

Defensive items are held items that reduce damage taken, improve defenses, or increase survivability in battle. 
They are commonly used to make Pokémon harder to defeat or to survive key attacks.

--------------------------------------------------
Main Defensive Items
--------------------------------------------------

Assault Vest:
- Increases Special Defense by 50%
- Prevents the use of status moves

Eviolite:
- Increases Defense and Special Defense by 50%
- Only works if the Pokémon can still evolve

Rocky Helmet:
- Deals damage to attackers when they make contact (1/6 of their max HP)

Air Balloon:
- Grants immunity to Ground-type moves
- Pops after the Pokémon is hit

Heavy-Duty Boots:
- Prevents damage and effects from entry hazards

Focus Band:
- Has a chance to survive a hit with 1 HP

--------------------------------------------------
Defensive Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- Defensive items do not usually increase damage output
- Many activate automatically without conditions
- Some items provide immunity to specific mechanics (e.g., hazards, Ground moves)
- Effects remain active as long as the item is held (unless consumed or removed)

--------------------------------------------------
Competitive Use
--------------------------------------------------

- Assault Vest is used to tank special attacks
- Eviolite is used on unevolved Pokémon to make them extremely bulky
- Heavy-Duty Boots are used to avoid hazard damage
- Air Balloon is used to temporarily remove Ground-type weaknesses
- Rocky Helmet punishes physical attackers
