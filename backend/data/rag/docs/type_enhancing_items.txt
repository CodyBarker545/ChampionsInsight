Type-Enhancing Items Guide

Type-enhancing items are held items that increase the power of moves of a specific type. 
They provide a consistent damage boost without drawbacks.

--------------------------------------------------
Effect
--------------------------------------------------

- Boost the power of moves that match a specific type
- Standard boost: 20% (1.2× damage)
- No negative side effects
- Always active while held

--------------------------------------------------
Mechanics
--------------------------------------------------

- Only affects moves of the matching type
- Stacks with STAB (Same-Type Attack Bonus)
- Does not lock the user into moves
- Does not cause recoil or drawbacks

--------------------------------------------------
Competitive Use
--------------------------------------------------

- Provides a safe damage boost without restrictions
- Often used when flexibility is more important than raw power
- Useful for Pokémon that need to switch moves frequently
- Alternative to Choice items when move locking is undesirable
